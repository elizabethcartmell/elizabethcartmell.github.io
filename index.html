Employment History

After completing her law degree at Durham University, Elizabeth moved into a financial role as Business Administrator in the Finance Hub at HM Prison Service. Ready for a challenge, she braved corridors and spearheaded an internal campaign to help the prison achieve financial break-even on a data project. She also met most the demanding clients of her career and tested her communication skills by explaining complex financial information to inmates who were illiterate and foreign nationals.

Leaving finance behind, she then moved to London to follow her passion for theatre. Now working at ATG Tickets, she works on a variety of different projects both in the UK and abroad, helping to facilitate projects and deliver results alongside supporting her team on a day-to-day basis.

Eizabeth puts her time to good use outside work by working on ad-hoc digital marketing and web development projects and receiving mentoring from industry professional to help develop her marketing skills.
