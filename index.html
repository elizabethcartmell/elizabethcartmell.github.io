Employment History

After completing her law degree at Durham University, Elizabeth moved into a financial role as Business Administrator in the Finance Hub within HM Prison Service. Always ready for a challenge, she braved prison corridors and spearheaded an internal campaign to help the prison achieve financial break-even on a data project. She also met most the demanding 'clients' of her career and tested her communication skills by explaining complex financial information to inmates who were often illiterate and foreign nationals.

Leaving finance behind, she then moved to London to follow her passion for theatre. Now working at ATG Tickets, she works on a variety of different projects both in the UK and abroad, helping to facilitate projects and deliver results alongside supporting her team on a day-to-day basis.
